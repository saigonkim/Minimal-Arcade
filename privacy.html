Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

At Minimal Arcade, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our games.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not require you to create an account for basic play. However, if you use Google Login or make a purchase, we may collect your email address and basic profile information provided by the authentication provider.

b. Game Data

We collect high scores, game progress, and achievements. This data is stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage) and synced to our servers if you are logged in.

c. Payment Data

Payments are handled exclusively by third-party processors (e.g., PayPal). We do not store your credit card or financial details on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Maintain global high-score leaderboards.
  • Validate and restore premium purchases.
  • Improve game performance and fix technical issues.

3. Local Storage and Cookies

We use LocalStorage to save your personal best scores and game preferences. This allows you to resume your progress even without an account. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For game data hosting and authentication.
  • PayPal: For secure payment processing.

These services have their own privacy policies which govern how they handle your data.

5. Your Rights

You can clear your local game data at any time by clearing your browser cache/storage. If you have a registered account and wish to delete your data from our servers, please contact us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

For privacy-related inquiries, please reach out via our Support channel.
