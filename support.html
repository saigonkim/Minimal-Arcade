Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Premium purchases like "Aero Odyssey Pro" are automatically linked to your browser's local storage. If you switch devices or clear your browser data, please use the "Restore Purchase" option in the game menu (Login required for persistent cross-device restoration).

The game is running slowly. What should I do?

Our games are built with high-performance HTML5 Canvas. Ensure you are using the latest version of Chrome, Safari, or Firefox. Closing other intensive browser tabs can also help improve frame rates.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! Minimal Arcade is fully responsive and optimized for touch interactions on iOS and Android devices.
